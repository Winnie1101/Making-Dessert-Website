Mooncake

step1

Mix together the ingredients for the dough.

Stir together the alkaline water, golden syrup, and vegetable oil, and sift in the flour more slowly. When mixed together, these ingredients should form a dough. Cover the dough with saran wrap and set it aside for at least 3 hours.

step2

Prepare the salted egg yolks and preheat the oven.

step3

Separate the dough and assemble your mooncake.
